There will be oppositions, but we should not fear because God will be with us and He will show himself strong. II Chronicles 15:7 (NIV) says, \"But as for you, be strong and do not give up, for your work will be rewarded.\" In the same way, the disciples were also afraid when they saw Jesus walking on the water, but Jesus said to them, \"Take courage!
